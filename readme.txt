The folder "GLE-V3" goes into the root of your SD card.

The riivolution/*.xml files only contain Memory Patches.
To make them work with your game, they still need editing,
such as Root, ID, Options, Choices and other Patches.

Read here for more information:
https://aerialx.github.io/rvlution.net/wiki/Patch_Format/

Your edited xml goes into the "riivolution" folder,
which should also exist on the root of your SD card.

Thanks for downloading!
